Proteomics

Expanding our multi-omic platform

Comprehensive proteomic capabilities complement our genomic platform, enabling a more complete molecular picture from your samples — from exploratory biomarker discovery through absolute quantification of specific proteins of interest.

Discovery Proteomics

Gather comprehensive proteomic insights from biological samples. Characterize protein-protein interactions, post-translational modifications, and differential protein expression to drive biomarker discovery and classifier development.

MALDI-TOF MS provides a window into the plasma proteome. Untargeted LC-MS/MS enables unbiased identification and quantification of thousands of targets in a single liquid biopsy, tissue, or cell culture sample.

  • MALDI-TOF mass spectrometry
  • Untargeted LC-MS/MS profiling
  • Protein-based classifier development
  • Next-generation spatial omics
  • Liquid biopsy, tissue & cell culture

Targeted Proteomics

Evaluate the abundance of proteins associated with your drug's mechanism of action or patient therapeutic response over time, with absolute quantification by targeted LC-MS/MS.

Assays are readily transferable to ELISA format for scalability and cost-effectiveness as your program advances toward larger cohorts.

  • Biomarker validation studies
  • MRD & drug response monitoring
  • Targeted protein degradation (TPD)
  • PKPD & cell signaling studies
  • Scalable ELISA transfer pathway
